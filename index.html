About

I'm Gagan Malvi, a student and a passionate custom ROM developer and Linux kernel fanboy. Founder and lead developer at the Cartel Project and Styx OS.

I'm currently part of team Paranoid Android. When I'm not on the internet I usually spend my time reading books or listening to music.

Work

The Cartel Project.

Founder.

We aim to unleash the actual capabilities and performance of your device, sometimes exceeding the limits the manufacturers set, with our custom kernel and ROM, Styx OS.

GitHub: Cartel Project

Styx OS

Founder and Lead Developer.

A minimalist aftermarket Android custom ROM, aiming to extend the life, performance, and subtle beauty of your device. Stable and timely updates with the latest security fixes, just like any other OEM.

GitHub: Styx Project

Paranoid Android

Official Maintainer.

Paranoid Android is a custom ROM aiming to extend the system, working on enhancing the already existing beauty of Android and following the same design philosophies that were set forward by Google for Android Open Source Project.
